About Us

Everyone should have access to clean water

Whether it’s a drink from the tap, a toilet that works, or a place to wash our hands, having water is a basic necessity that many of us take for granted,more than 840,000 people die each year from diseases contracted from unsafe water.

Building clean water and sanitation

Our goal is to bring water, sanitation, and hygiene services to everyone, and to make sure those solutions last forever.We spend years, not days, in districts – working until every family, clinic, and school has safe, reliable water and sanitation services.

Long-term thinking and lasting change

we’re a water charity that isn’t afraid to get our hands dirty. We have a realistic plan for achieving permanent change – a plan that involves working with communities, training local workers, building sustainable systems, and sharing what we’ve learned with the world.
